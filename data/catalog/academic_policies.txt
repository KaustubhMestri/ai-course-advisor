POLICY: Grading System - UC Berkeley 2024-2025

LETTER GRADES:
A+, A, A- = Excellent (4.0, 4.0, 3.7)
B+, B, B- = Good (3.3, 3.0, 2.7)
C+, C, C- = Satisfactory (2.3, 2.0, 1.7)
D+, D, D- = Passing (1.3, 1.0, 0.7)
F = Failing (0.0)

PREREQUISITE POLICY:
- Minimum grade of C required to use a course as prerequisite
- D grade does NOT satisfy prerequisite requirements
- Transfer course equivalencies must be approved by department advisor

REPEAT POLICY:
- Students may repeat a course maximum of 2 times
- Both grades appear on transcript
- Only the most recent grade counts toward GPA
- Department approval required for third attempt

CREDIT LIMITS:
- Maximum 20.5 credits per semester without dean approval
- Minimum 12 credits to be considered full-time
- Maximum 8 credits over summer session

CO-REQUISITE POLICY:
- Co-requisites must be taken concurrently in the same semester
- Cannot substitute a completed course for a co-requisite unless grade was B or above
- Exceptions require written instructor consent

ACADEMIC PROBATION:
- GPA below 2.0 results in academic probation
- Two consecutive semesters on probation may result in dismissal
