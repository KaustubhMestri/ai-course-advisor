PROGRAM: BS Computer Science - UC Berkeley
Total Credits Required: 120
Catalog Year: 2024-2025

REQUIRED LOWER DIVISION COURSES:
- CS61A: Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs
- CS61B: Data Structures
- CS61C: Machine Structures
- CS70: Discrete Mathematics and Probability Theory
- MATH1A: Calculus
- MATH54: Linear Algebra and Differential Equations

REQUIRED UPPER DIVISION COURSES:
- CS170: Efficient Algorithms and Intractable Problems
- CS186: Introduction to Database Systems
- CS188: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
- One additional upper division CS elective (300-level or above)

BREADTH REQUIREMENTS:
Students must complete one course from each of the following areas:
- Theory: CS170 or CS172
- AI/ML: CS188 or CS189
- Systems: CS186 or CS162
- Applications: Any 4xx CS course

ELECTIVES:
- 4 upper division CS electives required
- At least 2 must be from the approved CS elective list

RESIDENCY REQUIREMENT:
- Last 30 credits must be completed at UC Berkeley
- At least 6 upper division CS courses must be taken at UC Berkeley

GPA REQUIREMENT:
- Minimum 2.0 overall GPA required for graduation
- Minimum 2.0 GPA in upper division CS courses
