COURSE: CS61A - Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: None
Description: Introduction to programming and computer science.

COURSE: CS61B - Data Structures
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: CS61A (minimum grade C)
Description: Fundamental dynamic data structures, including linear lists, queues, trees, and other linked structures.

COURSE: CS61C - Machine Structures
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: CS61B and CS70
Description: Number representation, RISC-V assembly language, C programming, memory management.

COURSE: CS70 - Discrete Mathematics and Probability Theory
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: CS61A and MATH1A
Description: Logic, infinity, induction, stable matching, graphs, modular arithmetic, probability.

COURSE: CS170 - Efficient Algorithms and Intractable Problems
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: CS61B and CS70 and MATH54
Description: Design and analysis of algorithms. NP-completeness. Computational complexity.

COURSE: CS186 - Introduction to Database Systems
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: CS61B and CS61C
Description: Relational databases, SQL, query optimization, transactions, concurrency control.

COURSE: CS188 - Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: CS61B and CS70
Description: Search, constraint satisfaction, game trees, probabilistic models, machine learning.

COURSE: CS189 - Introduction to Machine Learning
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: CS188 and MATH54 and STAT134
Description: Supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, optimization.

COURSE: MATH1A - Calculus
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: None
Description: Introduction to differential and integral calculus.

COURSE: MATH54 - Linear Algebra and Differential Equations
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: MATH1A
Description: Vectors, matrices, eigenvalues, differential equations.

COURSE: STAT134 - Concepts of Probability
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: MATH1A
Description: Random variables, distributions, expectation, central limit theorem.
